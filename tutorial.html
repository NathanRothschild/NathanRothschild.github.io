Hello! Here is a short tutorial as to how you can make your map.


Step 1: Go to MapHub.net and create an account.


Step 2: Go to "New Map", and on the right side, set Visibility to Public. This is imperative. You may need to verify your email for this. Then, click Save.


Step 3: Add points to your map by clicking on the Point of Interest Icon on the left sidebar, and clicking where you want the point to be. You can also add a title, description, image, icon, or color to the point.


Step 4: After you have completed your map, click Save. Then, click on Map, and click on Link under Share. Copy that link.


Step 5: Send an email to CA900070781@concordacademy.org with the Subject as "MapHub Link (Your Name)", and with the content solely being the MapHub link.


You're done! Congratulations.
